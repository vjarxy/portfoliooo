GAME
DESIGN

Individual Project

ECHOES OF TALCHUM

Echoes of Talchum is a narrative-action 2D side-scroller rooted in folklore and traditional mask dance. The art direction combines vibrant hand-painted backgrounds, playful character sprites, and rhythmic combat that celebrates cultural motifs.

Date: 2023

VISUAL STYLE OF GAME ITEMS

Designed in-game assets inspired by Korean cultural heritage, featuring traditional masks, spirit figures, and symbolic icons. The visual style blends folklore authenticity with playful aesthetics to support the gameplay theme of capturing evil spirits.

DEMO APP LAYOUT & USER INTERFACE

The user interface of Echoes of Talchum combines traditional Korean cultural motifs with modern gameplay clarity. Each screen, from the start menu to the game over sequence, is designed to balance folklore-inspired visuals with intuitive navigation, creating an immersive yet accessible player experience.
